Kiwi Strawberry Smoothie

Description

This kiwi strawberry smoothie is full of tropical, fruity goodness!

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup pineapple and orange juice blend
  • 1/2 cup vanilla frozen yogurt
  • 1 banana
  • 6 strawberries
  • 1 kiwi

Steps

  1. Place juice blend, frozen yogurt, banana, strawberries, and kiwi into a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and pour into 2 glasses.
